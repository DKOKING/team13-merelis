We have created a game-themed like personality quiz that expresses you as a person.

This project's main focus is to make an avatar based on the users prefrences. The user has the choices of designing their avatar in the choice of skin color, hair and clothes. Then, the user has the option to take a quiz to determine their avatar's personality. After all of this, the user will have their final avatar and personality as a product from their choices and results. This projects purpose is to make a user friendly application combined with appEngine and datastore to create a personalized alter ego on our website.You will make your avatar through our Avatar Quiz and oce you are done with that you have to take our Personality Quiz.
